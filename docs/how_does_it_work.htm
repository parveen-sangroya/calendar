How Does it Work

 

 

First of all you choose a option from menu bar.

 

 If you want to Exit the Calendar click on ' = ' sign pop up menu will appear click on ' Exit ' .

 

 To check the Christian date of a year click on ' Christian ' in menu bar pop up will appear click on ' Year or Month ' and it will display the Calendar of that year or month.

 

 To check the Current year click on ' Current ' in menu bar pop up will appear click on ' Year or Month ' and it will display the Calendar of the year or month.

 

 To check the Shak date of a year click on ' Shak ' in menu bar pop up will appear click on ' Year or Month ' and it will display the Calendar of that year or month with Christian date.

 

 To check the Vikarmi date of a year click on ' Vikarmi ' in menu bar pop up will appear click on ' Year or Month ' and it will display the Calendar of that year or month with Christian date.

 

 To check the Holiday click on ' Holiday ' in menu bar pop up will appear click on ' Year or Month ' and it will display the Holiday of that year or month.

 

 To check the current date and time click on ' Timing ' in menu bar pop up will appear click on ' Date or Time ' it will display the date or time.

 

 At last for help click on ' Help 'in menu bar pop up will be appear click on ' Help it will display help.
