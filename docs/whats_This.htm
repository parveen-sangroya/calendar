CHRISTIAN :

This Calendar is daily used in life. Actually it is a  Gregorian Calendar and it's comes in INDIA.  When the Gregorian calendar was adopted  Britain in 1752, in this a correction of an 11-day discrepancy was made; the day after September 2, 1752, became September 14. The British also adopted January 1 as the day when a new year begins. We are use Gregorian Calendar at this time. In my Calendar all the changes are available and in this calendar a century years divisible evenly by 400 should be leap years and that all other century years should be common years. Thus, 1600 was a leap year, but 1700 and 1800 were common years.
